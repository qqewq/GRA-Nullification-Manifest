# GRA in Sociology

## Policy Simulation

Social systems as multi-agent GRA:
- $k=0$: Individual actions and transactions
- $k=1$: Group norms and local institutions
- $k=2$: National policy and culture
- $k=3$: Global governance frameworks

**Foam**: Policy contradictions, regulatory arbitrage, social unrest
**Nullification**: Participatory design, iterative policy refinement

## Conflict Resolution

Apply GRA to international disputes:
1. Capture positions of all parties (thought capture)
2. Identify shared and conflicting values (foam analysis)
3. Design nullification strategies (mediation)
4. Monitor stability post-agreement ($R_{	ext{hier}}$ tracking)
