# GRA in Linguistics

## Semantic Clustering

Word meanings as hierarchical foam:
- $k=0$: Phonetic/orthographic forms
- $k=1$: Lexical semantics
- $k=2$: Pragmatic and discourse meaning
- $k=3$: Cultural and philosophical connotations

**Foam**: Polysemy, ambiguity, translation mismatches
**Nullification**: Contextual disambiguation, semantic alignment

## Dialogue Coherence

Conversation as collaborative nullification:
- Each utterance updates the shared foam landscape
- Successful dialogue reduces mutual $\Phi$
- Misunderstanding = foam spike at some level

## Poetry and Meaning

Poetic compression as extreme nullification:
- Maximum meaning per unit of foam
- Metaphor = cross-level resonance transfer
- Rhythm = temporal nullification pattern
