# GRA in Physics

## Plasma Control

Plasma instabilities can be modelled as foam $\Phi$ in a hierarchical control system:
- $k=0$: Local magnetic field fluctuations
- $k=1$: Mode coupling and energy transfer
- $k=2$: Global confinement strategy

Nullification corresponds to real-time feedback control that dampens instabilities before they cascade.

## Quantum Analogs

The wave-particle duality in GRA (Section 6 of paper) mirrors quantum superposition:
- Wave-like state: Agent explores multiple hierarchy levels
- Particle-like state: Collapsed to a single stable goal

**Chiral Nullification**: A proposed extension where left/right-handed modes are nullified asymmetrically, analogous to chiral symmetry breaking.

## Cosmology

Large-scale structure formation can be viewed as hierarchical resonance:
- Dark matter halos → galaxy clusters → cosmic web
- Foam = gravitational potential fluctuations
- Nullification = virialisation (settling to equilibrium)
