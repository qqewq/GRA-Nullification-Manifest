# GRA in Chemistry

## Materials Discovery

The multiverse hypothesis testing from GRA can accelerate materials discovery:
- Each "universe" = a candidate material composition
- Foam = predicted instability (thermal, mechanical, electronic)
- Nullification = design rules that eliminate unstable candidates

## Reaction Pathways

Chemical reactions as hierarchical resonance:
- $k=0$: Molecular collisions (kinetics)
- $k=1$: Transition states (energy barriers)
- $k=2": Reaction network topology

**Foam**: Side reactions, catalyst poisoning
**Nullification**: Selective catalysis, reaction conditions optimisation

## Molecular Dynamics

Use GRA to coarse-grain molecular simulations:
- High-resolution atomistic model (high foam, high detail)
- Nullify fast degrees of freedom → mesoscale model
- Iterate to optimal resolution/foam trade-off
