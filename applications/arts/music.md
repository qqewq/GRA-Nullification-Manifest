- $k=0$: Individual notes (frequencies)
- $k=1$: Chords (simultaneous resonance)
- $k=2": Progressions (temporal resonance)

**Foam**: Dissonance, voice-leading violations, structural imbalance
**Nullification**: Resolution, cadence, return to tonic
