# GRA Manifest: The Nullification Game

## From 6200 Thoughts to Structured Reality

Every day, approximately **6200 thoughts** — micro-transitions between mental states — occur in your mind. These first reactions shape your subjective world.

## What is "Foam"?

**Foam** refers to internal contradictions that accumulate at different levels of the cognitive hierarchy:
- At the action level — conflicting habits
- At the emotional level — contradictory feelings
- At the value level — incompatible beliefs

## What is "Nullification"?

**Nullification** is the conscious resetting of destabilising resonances. It is not suppression, but recalibration to a more stable mode.

## How to Apply?

1. **Morning**: record your first thoughts (Angel-in-Pocket)
2. **Day**: note emotional reactions
3. **Evening**: cluster and nullify foam
4. **Night**: reflection and adjustment

## Two Layers

- **Personal**: Angel-in-Pocket — your mobile coach
- **Systemic**: GRA — architecture for agent swarms, organisations, and ASI
